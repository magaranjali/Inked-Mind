Whispers of Dawn

E
Emily Wright
Published on July 30, 2025
In the quiet moments before sunrise, When the world still dreams, I find myself in the spaces between Darkness and light. The whispers of dawn call to me, Soft tendrils of pink and gold Reaching across the sleeping sky, Promising renewal, promising hope. Dew drops glisten like scattered diamonds, Each one holding a universe within. The gentle breath of morning Carries secrets from yesterday. I stand at the threshold of becoming, As nature awakens around me, A symphony of birdsong and rustling leaves, Conducting the arrival of a new day. In this sacred space of transition, Where night surrenders to morning light, I too am transformed, reborn, Ready to begin again.
