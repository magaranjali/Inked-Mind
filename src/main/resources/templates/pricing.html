Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and local payment methods like eSewa and Khalti for our Nepalese users.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time and the new benefits will be available immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.

What happens to my poems if I cancel my subscription?

Your poems will remain on the platform, but you'll lose access to premium features. If you were on a paid plan and published more than the Free plan allows (5 poems/month), those poems will still be visible, but you won't be able to add new ones beyond the Free plan limit until you upgrade again.

Can I try Premium or Pro features before subscribing?

We occasionally offer free trials for new users. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when these promotions are available. Additionally, you can contact our support team for a feature demonstration.
